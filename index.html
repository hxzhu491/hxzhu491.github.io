About Me

I am a PhD in Biostatistics from The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston. I was advised by Dr. James Long and Dr. Kim-Anh Do from UT MD Anderson Cancer Center. My research focuses on Causal Inference and Statistical Machine Learning with applications in in-silico perturbation predictions and regulatory network inference.
